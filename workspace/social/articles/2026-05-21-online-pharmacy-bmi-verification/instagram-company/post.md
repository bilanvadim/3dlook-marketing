Our CEO asked ChatGPT to make her look 27 kg heavier. It took seconds.

That is the problem online pharmacies face right now. A patient uploads an AI-generated photo, passes a BMI check, and a real clinician never catches it.

A leading UK online pharmacy moved to a live, in-session body scan instead.

Here is how FitXpress works inside their order flow:
- Patient takes 2 photos inside the app — no camera roll access
- Liveness check confirms a real person in real time
- 80+ body measurements return in under 45 seconds
- BMI from the scan is checked against the patient's self-reported weight
- Mismatches get flagged before the prescription moves forward

The patient never sees the body data. The pharmacy gets a timestamped audit log ready for a regulator review.
