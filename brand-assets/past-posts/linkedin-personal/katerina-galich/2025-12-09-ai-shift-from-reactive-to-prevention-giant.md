Before symptoms. Before decline. Before it hurts.

As conversations at GIANT Health Event continue, one theme keeps rising to the surface:
AI is shifting healthcare from response to prevention.

When clinicians can:

- detect cardiovascular risk earlier,
- catch retinal decline before vision loss,
- validate GLP-1 pathways objectively rather than self-reported,
- support continuous care across borders,
care stops being a reaction - and becomes a prediction.
