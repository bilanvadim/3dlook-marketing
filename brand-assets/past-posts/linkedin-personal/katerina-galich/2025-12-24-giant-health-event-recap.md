The dust has finally settled after two very intense days at GIANT Health Event in London on 8–9 December, and I'm still thinking about the conversations more than the stages.

What I love most about events like GIANT is how many different parts of the healthcare ecosystem end up in one room: clinicians, founders, technologists, investors, media. You start to see how all the pieces can actually work together for the patient.

Back home now with a notebook full of ideas and even stronger motivation to keep building towards that future 💙
