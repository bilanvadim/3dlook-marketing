Insurance underwriting still relies heavily on forms, self-reported data, and manual review.

But body-related risk data is becoming more important in health, life, and wellness insurance and traditional collection methods create friction for both insurers and applicants.
📱 Mobile body scanning changes that.

With just two smartphone photos, AI-powered body scanning can generate body measurements, estimate body composition metrics, and provide structured health-related data in under a minute.
✅ No clinic visit
✅ No specialized hardware
✅ No manual measurements

For insurers, this means:
→ Faster underwriting workflows
→ More standardized data collection
→ Reduced dependency on self-reported information
→ Better remote onboarding experiences

For users, it creates a simpler and more accessible process that can happen from anywhere.
As digital underwriting evolves, the quality of body data collection will become a competitive advantage, especially for insurers building remote-first experiences.
