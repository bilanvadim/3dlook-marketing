# About Assel

Marketing professional with over 10 years of experience in B2C and B2B digital initiatives across international markets. Drives strategic growth through data-led research, analytics, high-impact content and digital execution.

## Editorial positioning (for blog bylines)

Assel is the default author for 3DLOOK blog articles and SEO content. Her byline applies to:

- Industry use-case deep-dives (insurance underwriting, online pharmacy BMI verification, employer wellness rewards, telehealth, online manufacturing)
- Buyer-facing comparison and buyer's-guide content
- Product deep-dives explaining how 3DLOOK technology works
- Trend pieces grounded in third-party data and operational implications

Assel's voice is **measured, analytical, B2B-trade-publication**. She writes for operators and compliance leads, not consumers. She hedges clinical claims, cites named external sources (NAIC, CDC, Swiss Re, McKinsey, Munich Re, LIMRA, Gen Re, BoF, ThredUp), and frames technology in workflow terms rather than feature terms.

See `brand-assets/style-guides/blog-style-guide.md` for the full voice specification derived from her existing articles.

## When NOT to use Assel as author

Founder-voice pieces — thought-leadership, personal experiments, opinion essays, conference reflections — are reserved for **Katerina Galich (CEO)**. Examples: the AI photo-manipulation experiment narrative, GLP-1 industry commentary, AI-risk strategy posts.

If a piece is grounded in personal experience, first-person reflection, or an industry-defining opinion, route it to Katerina. Everything else defaults to Assel.
