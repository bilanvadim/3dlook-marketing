# FitXpress ICP

## 1. Telehealth & GLP-1 / Weight Loss programs

**What they are:** virtual clinics, telehealth platforms, coaching apps treating obesity / weight management, GLP-1 prescription platforms.

**Buyer titles (target for outbound):**
- VP Product / Chief Product Officer
- Chief Medical Officer / Medical Director
- Head of Clinical Operations
- VP Engineering (when integration-driven)
- Head of Member Experience

**Company size:** Series A through public. 50-5000 employees. ARR $5M-$500M.

**Geo:** US primary, UK secondary, EU tertiary.

**KPIs they care about:**
- Member retention / 6-month adherence
- GLP-1 program ROI / cost-per-outcome
- Lean mass preservation tracking (clinical signal)
- Member engagement frequency

**Triggers (what makes them buy now):**
- Recent GLP-1 launch
- Pressure from payers to show outcomes
- Engagement metrics dropping after first 90 days
- Adding body composition (not just weight) to differentiate

**Examples to target:**
- Already in space: Ro, Hers, Found, Form Health, Calibrate, Noom, WeightWatchers Clinic
- Adjacent: Lyra, Hinge Health (for benchmarking only)

**Competitor in their evaluation:** Prism Labs (primary), Bodygram (secondary).

## 2. Online Pharmacies / Digital Prescribers (BMI Verification)

**What they are:** online pharmacies dispensing prescription weight-loss medications; need to verify eligibility (BMI threshold typically ≥27 with comorbidity or ≥30) without in-person visit.

**Buyer titles:**
- Head of Compliance / Chief Compliance Officer
- Chief Pharmacist / Superintendent Pharmacist (UK)
- VP Operations
- Head of Clinical Governance

**Company size:** 50-1000 employees. Public-funded or VC-backed.

**Geo:** UK strongest (regulatory pressure on online pharmacies), US growing.

**KPIs:**
- Compliance audit pass rate
- Eligibility screen accuracy
- Cost per qualified prescription
- Regulator complaint rate

**Triggers:**
- New regulator guidance on online weight-loss prescribing
- Inspection / investigation
- Adding GLP-1s to formulary

**Examples (live or relevant):**
- UK Meds (existing customer)
- Pharmacy2U, Boots Online, Pharmacy First (UK)
- Hims, Sesame Care, Push Health (US)

## 3. Life & Disability Insurance Underwriting

**What they are:** life insurers, disability insurers, group benefits carriers, digital insurance platforms, reinsurers — verify body metrics during application.

**Buyer titles:**
- VP Underwriting / Chief Underwriting Officer
- Head of Digital Innovation
- VP New Business
- Chief Risk Officer (for reinsurers)

**Company size:** large enterprise (typically 5K+ employees). Brand recognizable.

**Geo:** US (Prudential, MetLife, Guardian Life, New York Life, Lincoln Financial), EU (Aviva UK, Legal & General UK, Allianz DE, AXA FR, Generali IT).

**KPIs:**
- Underwriting cycle time (days)
- Application completion rate / drop-off
- Discrepancy / rework rate
- Audit readiness

**Triggers:**
- Digital transformation initiative
- Move to no-medical-exam underwriting tier
- Re-platforming / new policy admin system

**Hero message hook:** "Verify body metrics remotely to issue faster, cut rework, and strengthen auditability."

**Critical for outbound:** lead with HIPAA/GDPR + audit logs + workflow integration. NOT with accuracy. They care about defensibility.

## 4. Health Plans / Employer Wellness (Rewards Programs)

**What they are:** health insurers offering wellness incentive programs; employer benefit platforms; benefits administrators.

**Buyer titles:**
- VP Member Engagement
- Head of Wellness / Director of Wellbeing
- Chief Customer Officer
- VP Population Health

**Company size:** large enterprise.

**Geo:** US (UnitedHealthcare, Aetna/CVS, Cigna, Elevance, Kaiser Permanente), EU (Bupa UK — important reference!, Vitality UK, AXA Health, Allianz Partners).

**KPIs:**

- Check-in completion rate

**Triggers:**
- New plan year / wellness benefit refresh
- Pressure to prove ROI of wellness spend
- High dispute volume on self-reported progress

**Note:** Bupa Rewards is a public reference for what good looks like (see Bupa Rewards page).

## 5. Bariatric / Metabolic Clinics (Pre-Qualification)

**What they are:** bariatric surgery centers, hospital programs, multi-site surgical networks, metabolic / obesity clinics.

**Buyer titles:**
- Director of Operations
- Medical Director
- VP Patient Access
- Chief Operating Officer (multi-site)

**Company size:** mid-large hospital systems, surgical center chains.

**Geo:** US (HCA, Tenet, Ascension, CommonSpirit, Mayo Clinic), EU (Helios DE, Ramsay Santé FR, Spire UK, Nuffield Health UK, Asklepios DE).

- Long waitlist for consults
- High late-stage disqualification rate

## 7. Clinical Trials (CROs, Sponsors)

**Buyer titles:**
- Director of Clinical Operations
- VP Decentralized / Hybrid Trials
- Head of Site Management
- Director of Data Management

**Company size:** large (CROs) or specialized (DCT vendors).

**Geo:** global. US (IQVIA, Labcorp Drug Development, Syneos, Parexel, PPD/Thermo Fisher), EU (ICON Ireland, Eurofins, SGS Life Sciences, PSI CRO, Worldwide Clinical Trials).

- Site-to-site data variability
- Coordinator time per visit
- Retention / dropout rate

**Triggers:**
- New decentralized trial protocol
- Sponsor pushing for at-home capture
- Audit findings on measurement variability across sites

# Universal exclusion criteria

Across both products, do NOT pursue:
- Free / freemium consumer apps with no enterprise budget
- Companies under 50 employees unless they're a clear MTM tailor (small specialized brands OK)
- Companies in markets we're not licensed for compliance-wise (currently very strict on health data — US, UK, EU/EEA primarily)
- Companies that recently announced acquisition / merger (ICP shifting, sales cycle stalls)
- Existing customers (see `workspace/outbound/exclusions.md`)
