# Case Study: UK Meds — FitXpress

## Customer
UK Meds — UK-based online pharmacy.

## Use case
BMI verification for online pharmacy ordering — verifies eligibility before dispensing weight-loss medication.

## Numbers (2025)
- Scans: 7,500
- Customer lifetime to date: 7 months (still in early retention cycle)

## What they use
- FitXpress integrated as a verification step in checkout
- 2-photo scan triggered when user orders weight-loss treatment
- Smart Scales detects mismatch between self-reported weight and AI estimate

## Workflow pattern
**Pattern B (Customer workflow, no UI exposed to user)**:
- User submits photos
- Body data validates eligibility server-side
- User sees "Your photos are submitted" — body metrics never shown to user
- Compliance team has audit trail

## Why this matters (positioning)
- Strong reference for online pharmacy outbound
- Evidence of regulator-friendly compliance pattern
- Good white-label flexibility example

## How agents should use
- **In outbound to other online pharmacies (Pharmacy2U, Boots Online, Hims, etc.)**: cite as "UK Meds uses FitXpress for BMI verification."
- **In SEO articles about online pharmacy compliance**: example.
- **In compliance-focused outbound (insurance, regulated healthcare)**: example of audit-ready workflow.
