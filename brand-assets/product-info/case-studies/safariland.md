# Case Study: Safariland — Mobile Tailor

## Customer
Safariland — custom-fit PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) manufacturer and seller.

## Use case
Custom-fit PPE production. Measurement consistency to avoid remakes and reduce manufacturing costs.

## Numbers (2025)
- Scans: 15,500
- ARR: $256K
- Customer lifetime to date: 62 months (5+ years)

## What they use
- Mobile Tailor scanning integrated into customer ordering flow
- 80+ measurements feed pattern + manufacturing pipeline

## Outcomes (paraphrase carefully — confirm with Vadim before publishing)

- Faster order-to-delivery for distributed teams
- Sustained 5+ year partnership
