# Case Study: Yazen — FitXpress

## Customer
Yazen — weight loss management platform.

## Use case
Weight loss management support. Body composition tracking, visual progress, retention.

## Numbers (2025)
- Scans: 34,000 (highest scan volume of any FX customer)
- Customer lifetime to date: 7 months

## What they use
- FitXpress integrated into the patient app
- Periodic check-in scans for progress tracking
- 3D body visualization for engagement
- Body composition (BMI, fat %, lean mass) for clinical context

## Why this matters (positioning)
- High-volume use case — scaling story
- Direct evidence FitXpress works at member-engagement scale
- Reference for telehealth / weight loss / GLP-1 outbound

## How agents should use
- **In outbound to telehealth / weight loss platforms (Ro, Hers, Found, Calibrate, etc.)**: "Yazen uses FitXpress for member progress tracking, with 34K scans in 2025."
- Strong proof of scale.
- **In SEO articles about retention in weight-loss programs**: data point on body composition tracking.
