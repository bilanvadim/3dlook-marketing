# Case Study: Burlington Medical — Mobile Tailor

## Customer
Burlington Medical — custom-fit radiation protection apron manufacturer and seller.

## Use case
Custom-fit radiation apron production. Measurement consistency to avoid remakes, reduce manufacturing costs, and ensure proper protection fit.

## Numbers (2025)
- Scans: 11,500
- ARR: $309K
- Customer lifetime to date: 64 months (5+ years)

## What they use
- Mobile Tailor scanning integrated for healthcare worker measurement
- 80+ measurements feed apron customization

## Why this matters (positioning)
Radiation aprons that don't fit can compromise protection. Measurement precision is not just commercial — it's safety-critical. Strong story for medical device / safety-equipment outbound.

## How agents should use
- **In outbound to medical device / PPE manufacturers**: highlight Burlington's 64-month retention as evidence that the workflow integration is sticky.
- **In SEO articles**: example of safety-critical custom fit.
- **Never disclose ARR publicly.**
