# Case Study: Jim's Formal Wear — Mobile Tailor

## Customer
Jim's Formal Wear — formal wear rental and sales.

## Use case
Reduce manual measurement inconsistency to avoid returns. Enable remote measuring for wedding parties.

## Numbers (2025)
- Scans: 3,200
- ARR: $51K
- Customer lifetime to date: 58 months (~5 years)

## What they use
- Mobile Tailor for remote group measuring
- Wedding parties scan from their own phones, results feed customization for each member

## Why this matters (positioning)
- Strong story for use cases involving group / event measuring (weddings, corporate uniforms, team outfitting)
- Long retention (58 months) — proven stickiness

## How agents should use
- **In outbound to formal wear / event apparel companies**: cite as "Jim's Formal Wear has used Mobile Tailor for nearly 5 years for wedding party fittings."
- **In SEO content about distributed group measuring**: example.
