# Case Study: Generation Tux — Mobile Tailor

## Customer
Generation Tux — DTC online formal wear rental.

## Use case
Direct-to-consumer formal wear rental — body measurements at order time for proper fit on rentals.

## Numbers (2025)
- ARR: $77K (in early customer cycle)
- Customer lifetime to date: 1 month

## What they use
- Mobile Tailor scanning for rental customers
- Measurements feed sizing algorithm

## Why this matters (positioning)
- Modern DTC reference (vs. traditional formal wear chains)
- Newer customer — good signal of continued FX/MT acquisition
- Strong story for rental / circular fashion businesses

## How agents should use
- **In outbound to DTC fashion / rental businesses (Nuuly, Rent the Runway, Stitch Fix, etc.)**: cite as a recent reference.
- Don't lean too hard on this customer until lifetime grows past 12 months.
