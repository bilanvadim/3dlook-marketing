# Use Case: FitXpress — Online Pharmacy BMI Verification

## The pain we remove
Online pharmacies dispensing weight-loss medication rely on self-reported weight and BMI. Misuse risk, regulatory pressure, and audit findings.

## Why now
Regulators (especially UK GPhC, US FDA monitoring) are scrutinizing online weight-loss prescribing. Legitimate pharmacies need verifiable eligibility.

## What FitXpress is here
2-photo scan inside the order flow — verifies BMI before dispensing. Smart Scales detects mismatch between self-report and AI estimate.

## Hero message
Verify BMI inside the order flow to cut fraud, speed approvals, and stay audit-ready.

## ICP
Online pharmacies, digital prescriber platforms, telehealth-pharmacy hybrids.
**Buyers:** Head of Compliance, Chief Pharmacist, VP Operations, Head of Clinical Governance.

## Examples to target
UK Meds (existing customer — reference!), Pharmacy2U, Boots Online, Pharmacy First (UK), Hims, Sesame Care, Push Health (US).

## KPIs we improve
- Compliance audit pass rate
- Eligibility screen accuracy
- Cost per qualified prescription
- Regulator complaint rate
- Drop-off in checkout (improve via simple flow)

## Commercial value
Risk reduction (regulatory + reputational) + lower ops cost (less manual review).

## AI resilience
Hard to replace — we provide the workflow integration that ties scan → eligibility decision → audit log. Apple/Google won't ship that.

## Critical messaging
Lead with compliance and audit-readiness, not accuracy. These buyers care about defensibility under scrutiny.

## Reference customer
**UK Meds** uses FitXpress for BMI verification. Use this as a "trusted by" data point in outbound to similar pharmacies — but never disclose specific results without permission.
