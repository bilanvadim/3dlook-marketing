# Use Case: Mobile Tailor — Uniform Fitting

## The pain we remove
Uniform companies fitting large groups (workwear, healthcare, public safety, military) face high alteration rates, slow fitting events, and complex logistics — especially across distributed sites.

## What Mobile Tailor is here
Measure large groups remotely. Each member of the group scans on their own phone — measurements feed manufacturing pipeline.

## Hero message
Measure large groups remotely to reduce fitting events, alterations, and order-to-delivery time.

## ICP
Uniform companies, PPE manufacturers, workwear suppliers, healthcare uniform vendors, public safety / defense outfitters.
**Buyers:** VP Operations, Director of Procurement, Head of Customer Solutions.

## Reference customers
- **Safariland** — custom-fit PPE manufacturer (15.5K scans/year, 62 months retention, $256K ARR). Use case: measurement consistency to avoid remakes, reduce manufacturing costs.
- **Burlington Medical** — custom-fit radiation aprons (11.5K scans/year, 64 months retention, $309K ARR).
- **Redthread** — custom alterations for sports team uniforms.
- **Warrior Consultants** — custom equipment fitting.

## KPIs we improve
- Fitting event time / cost (often eliminated entirely)
- Alteration rate
- Order-to-delivery time for groups
- Per-unit measurement cost across distributed teams

## Commercial value
Capacity and margin: skip in-person fittings, scale to thousands of members without proportional increase in measuring staff.

## Why we win
- Long retention proven (62-64 months at Safariland and Burlington Medical)
- Stable measurement output that integrates into custom manufacturing
- White-label flexibility — uniform companies can integrate into their existing portals
