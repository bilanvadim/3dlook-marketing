# Use Case: Mobile Tailor — Wrist & Limb Measurement (New)

## The pain we remove
Wearables, jewelry, and medical device manufacturers struggle with sizing — returns due to poor fit, customer dissatisfaction, suboptimal medical device performance.

## What Mobile Tailor is here
Precise wrist + limb measurements (best-in-class accuracy: wrist girth at 0.54 cm absolute error). Fit-aware product personalization at scale.

## Hero message
Precise wrist & limb measurements for products that depend on perfect fit.

## ICP
- **Wearables manufacturers** — smartwatches, fitness trackers, health monitors
- **Jewelry brands** — bracelets, rings, watches (custom fit + sizing)
- **Medical device manufacturers** — prosthetics, post-surgery devices, braces, supports

**Buyers:** VP Product, CPO, Head of Customization, Director of Patient Fit (medical), Director of Product Customization (jewelry).

## KPIs we improve
- Customer satisfaction (fewer returns due to fit)
- Conversion rate (personalized fit → higher purchase intent)
- Healing / device performance (medical devices)

## Why this is differentiated
Wrist girth is our highest-accuracy measurement (0.54 cm absolute error) — better than many use cases. Strong story for precision-fit products.

## Commercial value
- For jewelry: convert "browse and bounce" buyers into committed buyers via personalized fit visualization
- For wearables: reduce return rate from sizing issues (single biggest return reason for wrist devices)
- For medical: improve device fit which improves outcome — clinical adherence

## AI resilience
Specialized accuracy + integration into product configurator + commerce flow. Not replaceable by generic OS measurement primitive.
