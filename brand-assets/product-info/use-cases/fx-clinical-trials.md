# Use Case: FitXpress — Clinical Trials (Metabolic/Obesity, Hybrid/DCT)

## The pain we remove
Multi-site trials rely on manual measurements that vary by site/staff. Inconsistent data, extra coordinator workload, dropout from "measurement-only" visits.

## Why now
Trials shifting to hybrid models with remote check-ins for recruitment and retention. Sponsors and CROs need standardized, time-stamped capture that supports monitoring and audits.

## What FitXpress is here
Scan-based capture of standardized body measurements + BMI/body composition across sites. Optional remote check-ins (structured, time-stamped).

## Hero message
Standardize anthropometrics across sites and reduce visit burden to improve data quality and retention.

## ICP
CROs, pharma sponsors, academic research networks, decentralized clinical trial platforms.
**Buyers:** Director of Clinical Operations, VP Decentralized Trials, Head of Site Management, Director of Data Management.

## Examples to target
**Global:** IQVIA, Labcorp Drug Development, Syneos Health, Parexel, PPD (Thermo Fisher).
**EU:** ICON (IE), Eurofins (EU), SGS Life Sciences (CH/EU), PSI CRO (CH), Worldwide Clinical Trials.

## KPIs we improve
- Site-to-site variability (more consistent data)
- Coordinator time per visit
- Retention / fewer measurement-only visits
- Screen failure rate
- Audit readiness

## Market sizing
**TAM (global):** $10-40M / year (20-80M scan events × ~$0.50)
**SAM (US + EU/UK near-term):** $3-12M / year

## Commercial value
Lower per-participant operational cost + higher data quality. Repeatable across studies and sponsors.

## AI resilience
We sell standardization + auditability (QA, monitoring, traceable records). OS features don't replace protocol workflows + sponsor-grade documentation.
