# Use Case: FitXpress — Telehealth & GLP-1 / Weight Loss

## The pain we remove
Programs rely on self-reported weight and inconsistent check-ins, which weakens engagement, makes ROI hard to prove, and exposes GLP-1 programs to lean-mass loss without monitoring.

## Why now
GLP-1 demand is surging. Payers are pressuring programs to show outcomes. Body composition (not just weight) is the new differentiator.

## What FitXpress is here
2-photo scan inside the host app — body composition + 80+ measurements + 3D progress visualization. Smart Scales detects self-reported weight mismatch.

## Hero message
Verify body progress remotely to boost retention, reduce drop-off, and prove program ROI.

## ICP
Virtual clinics, telehealth platforms, coaching apps, GLP-1 prescription platforms.
**Buyers:** VP Product, CMO, Head of Clinical Operations, VP Engineering, Head of Member Experience.

## Examples to target
Ro, Hers, Found, Form Health, Calibrate, Noom, WeightWatchers Clinic.

## KPIs we improve
- 6-month retention
- GLP-1 program ROI / cost-per-outcome
- Lean mass preservation tracking
- Member engagement frequency
- Drop-off in months 2-3 (the danger zone)

## Commercial value
Higher retention drives LTV. Visualized progress drives engagement. Lean-mass preservation is a clinical differentiator.

## AI resilience
We sell a workflow (longitudinal tracking + 3D goal visualization + Smart Scales fraud detection), not raw measurements. Apple/Google primitives don't replace this layer.

Prism Labs (primary), Bodygram (secondary).
