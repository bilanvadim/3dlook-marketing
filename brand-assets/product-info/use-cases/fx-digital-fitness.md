# Use Case: FitXpress — Connected & Digital Fitness

## The pain we remove
Digital fitness platforms struggle with progress visualization beyond weight + workout count. Engagement plateaus after first 90 days.

## What FitXpress is here
3D body model + 80+ measurements + side-by-side progress comparison + goal weight visualization — embedded in the platform.

## Hero message
Personalize training plans and visualize real progress with 3D scans.

## ICP
Digital fitness platforms, connected fitness apps, coaching subscriptions.
**Buyers:** Head of Product, VP Engagement / Retention, CPO.

## Examples to target
Peloton (digital), Fitbod, Future, Tonal, Whoop (adjacent), Caliber, Trainerize.

## KPIs we improve
- Subscription retention
- DAU / WAU
- Trainer-to-member ratio efficiency

## Commercial value
Higher LTV via retention. Differentiation in crowded fitness app market.

## AI resilience
We sell the engagement workflow (3D goal viz + progress comparison + body data nutrition) layered on accuracy. Features bundled tightly with retention metrics survive commoditization.
