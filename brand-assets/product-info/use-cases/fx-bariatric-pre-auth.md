# Use Case: FitXpress — Bariatric Pre-Qualification & Pre-Auth Documentation

## The pain we remove
Eligibility is confirmed too late. Pre-auth documentation is slow/manual, causing delays, cancellations, wasted consult slots.

## Why now
Demand for bariatric care is growing, clinics are capacity-constrained. Programs need faster intake + cleaner docs without measurement-only visits.

## What FitXpress is here
2-photo scan before the consult — captures objective body metrics + BMI/body composition (structured, time-stamped) for early screening + documentation.

## Hero message
Pre-qualify patients remotely to reduce wasted consults, speed pre-auth, and improve conversion to procedures.

## ICP
Bariatric surgery centers, hospital programs, multi-site surgical networks, metabolic/obesity clinics.
**Buyers:** Director of Operations, Medical Director, VP Patient Access, COO (multi-site).

## Examples to target
**US:** HCA Healthcare, Tenet Healthcare, Ascension, CommonSpirit Health, Mayo Clinic.
**EU:** Helios (DE), Ramsay Santé (FR), Spire Healthcare (UK), Nuffield Health (UK), Asklepios (DE).

## KPIs we improve
- Consult-to-procedure conversion rate
- Late-stage disqualifications / cancellations
- Pre-auth cycle time
- Staff time per pre-auth packet

## Market sizing
**TAM (US + EU/UK):** $10-30M / year (20-60M pathway scans × ~$0.50)
**SAM (near-term target networks):** $2-8M / year

## Commercial value
Higher conversion to procedures + faster authorization + lower intake/pre-auth workload.

## AI resilience
We sell workflow efficiency + documentation (exports + audit-ready records). OS features don't replace the process layer.
