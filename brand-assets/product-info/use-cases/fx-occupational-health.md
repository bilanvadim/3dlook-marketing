# Use Case: FitXpress — Occupational Health Pre-Employment / Return-to-Work Screening

## The pain we remove
High-volume screening still relies on manual measurements + inconsistent intake — clinic bottlenecks, rescreens, uneven documentation across sites/vendors.

## Why now
Employers hiring at scale, occupational health providers are capacity-constrained. Programs need faster throughput + standardized documentation without extra appointments.

## What FitXpress is here
2-photo scan before appointments — standardized body measurements + BMI/body composition (structured, time-stamped). Reduces manual intake.

## Hero message
Standardize screening intake remotely to increase throughput, reduce rescreens, and speed clearance decisions.

## ICP
Occupational health providers, workforce screening vendors, workers' comp / absence administrators, large multi-site employers.
**Buyers:** VP Operations, COO, CMO, Director of Clinical Services, Head of Occupational Health.

## Examples to target
**US:** Concentra, Premise Health, Marathon Health, Optum (occupational services), US HealthWorks.
**EU:** International SOS, Medicover, Bupa Occupational Health (UK), Nuffield Health (UK), AXA Assistance.

## KPIs we improve
- Appointment duration → throughput (candidates / day)
- Rescreen / rework rate
- Time-to-clearance
- Documentation quality across sites (audit-friendly)

## Market sizing
**TAM (US + EU/UK):** $20-60M / year (40-120M scan events × ~$0.50)
**SAM (near-term networks):** $5-15M / year

## Commercial value
Lower screening cost per candidate + higher clinic throughput. High-volume, repeatable deployments.

## AI resilience
We sell throughput + consistency at scale (workflow rules, QA, reporting). OS features don't replace operational layer + integrations.
