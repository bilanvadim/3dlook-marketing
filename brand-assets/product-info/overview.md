# 3DLOOK — Overview

## What we do (one sentence)

3DLOOK turns two smartphone photos into a 3D body model and 80+ body measurements in 45 seconds, via API and SDK.

## How we deliver value

We are NOT just a measurement API. We are a **trusted workflow layer** for industries where verified body data drives business outcomes — measurement consistency, fraud reduction, faster underwriting, better retention, audit-ready documentation.

Our differentiation lives in three places:
1. **Outcomes, not measurements** — every product ties to a measurable customer KPI (retention, conversion, CPL, time-to-clearance)
2. **Workflow integration** — longitudinal tracking, audit logs, consent management, deep API/SDK integration that's costly to swap
3. **Reliability + governance** — confidence scoring, drift monitoring, HIPAA/GDPR, on-device options for privacy

## Two products

### FitXpress (Health & Fitness)
2-photo scan inside a host app to capture verified BMI, body composition, and 80+ measurements. Sold to telehealth, insurance, weight-loss programs, online pharmacies, employer wellness, occupational health, clinical trials.

Hero benefits: anti-fraud (Smart Scales detects self-report mismatch), engagement (3D goal visualization), workflow efficiency (automated onboarding/validation), audit-ready records.

### Mobile Tailor (Apparel & Uniforms)
Same scanning tech, optimized for precise garment fit. Sold to MTM brands, on-demand manufacturers, uniform companies, custom alterations.

Hero benefits: measurement consistency to reduce remakes/returns, remote measuring at scale, integration into pattern-making and OMS.

## Strategic context (read before positioning)

Computer vision is commoditizing in 12-36 months. Apple/Google may release native body/measurement primitives. Differentiation shifts from "best model" to **outcomes + workflow + governance**. Don't sell accuracy; sell business defensibility.
